1 What is Dynamics 365 Field Service?

Dynamics 365 Field Service helps organizations deliver service at the customer's location — installations, maintenance, and repairs. The central record is the work order, which describes the work a technician must perform on site.

From issue to on-site fixField Service connects the back-office (the issue) to the field (the fix): scheduling the right person, with the right parts, at the right time.

2 The work order lifecycle

  1. A work order is generated from a case, sales order, agreement, email, or IoT signal.
  2. It is scheduled — a resource (technician) is assigned by the scheduler.
  3. The technician travels on site and performs the work using the mobile app.
  4. Parts (inventory) and time are recorded against the work order.
  5. The work order is completed and reviewed; it feeds billing and analytics.

3 Scheduling & resources

Universal Resource Scheduling assigns the best resource to each work order based on skills, location, and availability — manually, with assistance, or fully automated.
