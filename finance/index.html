1 What is Dynamics 365 Finance?

Dynamics 365 Finance helps enterprises manage their financials with accurate, real-time information for planning and forecasting. It automates core accounting and gives finance teams the controls, compliance, and reporting that large, multi-entity organizations require.

Part of Finance & OperationsFinance is one of the enterprise ERP apps (with Supply Chain Management), running on the Finance & Operations platform rather than directly on Dataverse.

2 The general ledger

The general ledger (GL) is the heart of Finance — the central record of all financial transactions, organized by a chart of accounts. Subledgers (like AP and AR) post their detail up into the GL.

ConceptRole
Chart of accountsThe structured list of all GL accounts.
LedgerThe book of financial record for a legal entity.
SubledgerDetailed ledgers (AP, AR) that roll into the GL.
Fiscal calendarThe periods financial results are reported in.

3 Accounts payable & receivable

Accounts payable (AP)

Money you owe suppliers: track vendors, invoices, and payments out.

Accounts receivable (AR)

Money owed to you: track customers, invoices, and payments in.
The cash-flow pairAP and AR are mirror images — together they give finance a real-time view of cash moving out and in.

4 Budgeting

Budgeting sets financial targets and controls spend against them. Basic budgeting tracks planned vs. actual; budget control can stop or warn on transactions that exceed the budget.

  • Create budgets by account, department, or project.
  • Compare actuals to budget for variance analysis.
  • Apply budget control to enforce limits at point of spend.
