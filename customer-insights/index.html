1 What is Dynamics 365 Customer Insights?

Dynamics 365 Customer Insights has two connected capabilities: Data (a customer data platform that unifies fragmented data into one profile) and Journeys (real-time marketing that orchestrates personalized customer journeys).

Customer Insights – Data

Ingest and unify data from many sources into a single customer profile.

Customer Insights – Journeys

Design real-time, triggered journeys across email, SMS, and more.
Profiles power everythingUnified profiles and segments built in Data become the audiences you target in Journeys.

2 Unifying customer data

  1. Ingest data from CRM, ERP, web, loyalty, and more.
  2. Match & merge records that refer to the same person.
  3. Produce a unified profile — one trusted view of each customer.
  4. Enrich with measures and AI predictions (e.g. churn likelihood).

3 Segments

Segments group customers by demographic, transactional, or behavioral attributes. Profiles matching a segment's filters are its members, and become the audience for campaigns, sales, and support.

Start from a questionBuild a segment to answer a business question — "high-value customers who lapsed in 90 days" — then target it with a journey.

4 Real-time journeys

Journeys react to customer behavior as it happens. A trigger (a signup, a purchase, an abandoned cart) starts a flow that sends the right message on the right channel at the right moment.

Trigger-based

Start journeys from real-time events, not just batch lists.

Multichannel

Email, SMS, push, and custom channels in one flow.

Personalized

Content adapts to the individual profile and behavior.
