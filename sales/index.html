1 What is Dynamics 365 Sales?

Dynamics 365 Sales helps sellers build relationships, act on insights, and close deals faster. It tracks the journey from a first lead through a qualified opportunity to a won deal — all on the shared Dataverse customer record.

Copilot built inCopilot summarizes leads and opportunities, catches you up on recent changes, prepares you for meetings, and enriches records with external information.

2 The core sales flow

  1. A lead arrives (web form, event, referral) — an unqualified prospect.
  2. You qualify the lead, confirming interest, budget, and fit.
  3. It becomes an opportunity — a real potential deal with products and value.
  4. You progress it through pipeline stages, logging activities.
  5. You close the opportunity as won or lost; won deals can become orders.
Lead vs opportunityA lead is a maybe; an opportunity is a qualified deal you're actively working. Qualifying converts one to the other.

5 Pipeline & forecasting

The pipeline is your set of open opportunities by stage; forecasting rolls their value into a predicted revenue number you can manage against quota.

  • Track each opportunity's stage, value, and close date.
  • Use the pipeline view to spot stalled or at-risk deals.
  • Roll up forecasts by seller, team, or territory.
