1 What is Business Central?

Dynamics 365 Business Central is an all-in-one business management solution built and optimized for small and medium businesses. It's for companies that have outgrown entry-level accounting software and want finance, sales, purchasing, inventory, and operations in one connected app.

One app, not manyWhere enterprises run separate Finance and Supply Chain apps, Business Central bundles those capabilities into a single, simpler product sized for SMBs.

2 What it covers

Finance

General ledger, banking, cash flow, and reporting.

Sales & receivables

Quotes, orders, invoices, and customer balances.

Purchasing & payables

Vendor orders, invoices, and payments.

Inventory

Items, stock levels, and valuation.

Projects

Jobs, time sheets, and project costing.

Operations

Light manufacturing, service, and warehousing.

3 Finance in Business Central

Finance is the core: a chart of accounts, general ledger, and the receivables/payables that track money in and out — the same accounting concepts as enterprise Finance, in a streamlined package.

  • Post and review entries against the general ledger.
  • Manage customer and vendor invoices and payments.
  • Reconcile bank accounts and report on cash flow.

4 Connect & extend

Business Central integrates tightly with Microsoft 365 (work in Outlook and Excel) and the Power Platform, and extends through AppSource apps and per-tenant extensions.

Start with the base, add as you growBegin with the standard app, then add AppSource extensions for industry-specific needs instead of customizing the core.
