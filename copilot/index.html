Copilot & AI in Dynamics 365

Copilot and AI agents woven through every Dynamics 365 app — helping sellers, agents, and finance teams work faster with grounded, in-context assistance.

1 Copilot & AI in Dynamics 365

Every Dynamics 365 app now has Copilot built in — AI assistance grounded in your business data. Because the apps share Dataverse, Copilot reasons over the same records your teams work in, in the context of the task at hand.

Grounded in your dataDynamics 365 Copilot answers from your records (accounts, cases, opportunities) — not just general knowledge — so its help is specific and actionable.

2 What Copilot does, app by app

AppCopilot helps you
SalesSummarize leads/opportunities, prep for meetings, draft emails.
Customer ServiceDraft responses, summarize cases, answer from knowledge.
Field ServiceCreate and recap work orders; assist technicians.
FinanceSurface insights and assist with collections and analysis.
Customer InsightsBuild segments and journeys with natural language.

3 From Copilot to agents

Beyond in-app assistance, you can build agents that act autonomously on Dynamics 365 data — for example a sales-qualification agent or a case-resolution agent — using Copilot Studio on the same Dataverse foundation.

In-app Copilot

Assists a user with the task in front of them.

Autonomous agents

Carry out multi-step work, triggered by events or schedules.

Built on Dataverse

Agents reason over the same governed business records.
Same platform as Microsoft IQThese agents connect to the wider Microsoft agent ecosystem — Copilot Studio, Work IQ, and Agent 365 — covered in the main Training & Development library.

4 Trust & control

  • Copilot honours each user's existing Dataverse permissions.
  • Admins control which Copilot features are enabled per app and environment.
  • Responses stay grounded in your tenant's data and governance.
  • AI capabilities follow Microsoft's responsible-AI commitments.
